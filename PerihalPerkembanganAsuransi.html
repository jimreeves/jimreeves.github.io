Perihal Perkembangan Asuransi


Perlindungan Maksimal dari Allianz Allisya Protection Plus beema vitteey nukasaan se suraksha ka saadhan hai. yah jokhim prabandhan ka ek roop hai, mukhy roop se ek aakasmik ya anishchit haani ke jokhim ke khilaaph bachaav ke lie upayog kiya jaata hai. beema pradaan karane vaalee ek ikaee beemaakarta, beema kampanee, beema vaahak ya andararaitar ke roop mein jaanee jaatee hai. ek vyakti ya ikaee jo beema khareedatee hai use beemaadhaarak ya poliseedhaarak ke roop mein jaana jaata hai. beema lenaden mein beemit vyakti ko kavar kie gae nukasaan kee sthiti mein beemit vyakti ko kshatipoorti karane ke lie beemaakarta ke vaade ke badale mein beemaakarta ko bhugataan ke roop mein gaaranteekrt aur gyaat apekshaakrt kam nukasaan maana jaata hai. nukasaan vitteey ho sakata hai ya nahin bhee ho sakata hai, lekin yah vitteey sharton ke lie kamajor hona chaahie, aur aamataur par usamen kuchh shaamil hota hai jisamen beemit vyakti ke svaamitv, kabje ya poorvavartee sambandhon dvaara sthaapit beema yogy byaaj hota hai. beemaadhaarak ko ek beema praapt hota hai, jise beema polisee kaha jaata hai, jo sharton aur paristhitiyon ka vivaran deta hai jisake tahat beemaakarta beemit vyakti kee kshatipoorti karega. Perihal Perkembangan Asuransi

Perihal Perkembangan Asuransi beema polisee mein ullikhit kavarej ke lie beemit vyakti ko beemaakarta dvaara lagae gae paise kee raashi preemiyam kaha jaata hai. agar beemaadhaarak ko nukasaan ka anubhav hota hai jo sambhaavit roop se beema polisee dvaara kavar kiya jaata hai, to beemit vyakti beemaakarta ko daave samaayojak dvaara prasanskaran ke lie daava prastut karata hai. beemaakarta punarmilan karake apana jokhim bacha sakata hai, jisase ek aur beema kampanee kuchh jokhim le jaane ke lie sahamat hotee hai, khaasakar yadi praathamik beemaakarta ise le jaane ke lie jokhim ko bahut bada maanata hai. jokhim ko sthaanaantarit karane ya vitarit karane ke tareeke cheenee aur bebeeloniyan vyaapaariyon dvaara kramashah teesare aur 2 sahasraabdee eesa poorv ke roop mein abhyaas kie gae the. cheenee vyaapaariyon ne vishvaasaghaatee nadee raipids yaatra karane ke lie kaee jahaajon mein apane maal ko phir se vitarit kar diya taaki kisee bhee jahaaj ke kaipsing ke kaaran nukasaan seemit ho sake. . baabuliyon ne ek pranaalee vikasit kee jise prasiddh hammuraabee, see mein darj kiya gaya tha. 1750 eesa poorv, aur praarambhik bhoomadhy naukaayan vyaapaariyon dvaara abhyaas kiya.
